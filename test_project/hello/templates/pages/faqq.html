FAQ Page

What is Webble?
Webble is a weather app that uses the Dark Skies API to provide the user (you) with current and weekly weather for any city in the USA.
What is a weather call and why are they limited?
Whenever you refresh the weather, you are making a call. There is a limit because Dark Skies places a limit on us (the developers).
What is the differences between a free and premium account?
Free users can only store one location while Premium users can store up to five cities. Free users can call for the weather 33 times per day and Premium users can make 66 calls per day.
How do I get a Premium account?
When you sign up, just check the "Make my account Premium" box. There is no charge.
What if my city isn't listed?
Google the Latitude and Longitude and input those into the location boxes
Can I get Webble on my phone?
No. This is a web app only
